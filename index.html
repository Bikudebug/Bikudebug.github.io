About Me

I’m a Project Assistant-I at IIT Gandhinagar (Electrical Engineering). My work focuses on human motion understanding using pose data, unsupervised learning, and spatio-temporal modeling. I hold an M.Sc. in Physics from IITGN and transitioned into computer vision research. My passion is developing models that learn from minimal supervision and explain complex sports/human dynamics. Currently exploring multimodal learning and vision-language models for biomechanical phase analysis.

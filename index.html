About Me

I’m a recent Project Assistant-I at IIT Gandhinagar (Electrical Engineering). My work focuses on understanding human motion using pose data, unsupervised learning techniques, and spatio-temporal modeling.

I hold a Master’s degree in Physics from IIT Gandhinagar, where I transitioned into the field of computer vision through hands-on research and project experience.

My passion lies in developing models that learn from minimal supervision and make sense of complex visual dynamics, especially in sports and human-centric videos. Currently, I’m exploring multimodal learning and vision-language models for action captioning, with a focus on biomechanical phases in throwing sports.
